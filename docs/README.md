# OctoAcme Project Management Documentation

## Project Management Processes Summary

OctoAcme follows a structured project management lifecycle to deliver customer value efficiently and adapt to changing requirements. Our approach includes:

1. **Initiation** – Validate business need, align stakeholders, and confirm go/no-go decision
2. **Planning** – Break work into shippable increments, identify dependencies and risks, and establish release timelines
3. **Execution & Tracking** – Manage day-to-day delivery, quality assurance, and progress reporting
4. **Risk & Communication Management** – Identify and mitigate risks, maintain stakeholder alignment, and escalate blockers
5. **Release & Deployment** – Standardize how we ship features to production with safety and observability
6. **Retrospectives & Continuous Improvement** – Capture learnings and convert them into actionable improvements

Key principles across all phases:
- **Customer-first**: Prioritize customer value and usability
- **Iterative delivery**: Deliver small, testable increments
- **Clear ownership**: Each project has a named Project Manager (PM) and Product Lead
- **Data-informed decisions**: Measure impact and iterate based on evidence
- **Psychological safety**: Encourage feedback and learning
